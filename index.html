WHO AM I?

How?
For more than 10 years, I've been working as a visual artist and freelance graphic designer, creating designs and illustrations for various individuals and organizations, while also going through school to achieve one of my goals of becoming a lawyer. Now a Barrister & Solicitor of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, with a specialization in corporate, entertainment, intellectual property, real estate, contracts, wills and probate law practices, I set my sights on achieving yet another goal of getting into tech. With the longest interest in web design, I signed up for a remote tech bootcamp, learnt to code, developed numerous websites, collaborated on several projects and earned an internationally-recognized certificate in Front-End Engineering from the industry-renowned Vephla University. This is my portfolio.
